## Let's Summarize

- The relationship between a categorical explanatory variable and a
                        quantitative response variable is summarized using:
  - *Data display:* Side-by-side boxplots
  - *Numerical summaries:* Descriptive statistics
- Exploring the relationship between a categorical explanatory variable and a
                        quantitative response variable amounts to comparing the distributions of the
                        quantitative response for each category of the explanatory variable. In
                        particular, we look at how the distribution of the response variable differs
                        between the values of the explanatory variable.
